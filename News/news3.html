MineTrade Interactive Expands Game Portfolio

September 11, 2025 | Author: MineTrade Writing Team

MineTrade Interactive continues to grow its presence in the gaming world with new projects and exciting updates for our community. Our focus remains on delivering innovative, immersive, and high-quality experiences to players on PC and beyond.

In this update, we’re highlighting our latest game in development: KickFlare. This 2D single-player football game is currently in Alpha and features the exclusive "KickFlare" mode. Players will face challenging AI opponents on a fast-paced 2D pitch, testing both strategy and reflexes.

We are committed to gathering feedback from early access players to improve the game. The Alpha stage is crucial for refining mechanics, balancing gameplay, and ensuring a polished full release in the future.

Community & Testing

Early testers of KickFlare have the unique opportunity to provide feedback that directly influences game development. By engaging with the community, MineTrade Interactive ensures that each release meets the expectations of our players and delivers an enjoyable experience.

Alpha testers are encouraged to report any bugs, suggest enhancements, and share gameplay experiences. This collaborative approach helps us iterate quickly and deliver a game that players will love.

Future updates will expand upon the current KickFlare mode, including additional features, modes, and enhancements. Stay tuned for new announcements as development progresses.
