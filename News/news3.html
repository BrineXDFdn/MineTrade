✨ We've Polished Our Website for the Better ✨

June 27, 2026 · Author: MineTrade Interactive

We've given MineTradeInteractive.com a full pass top to bottom. Same pages, same links, same games — just a cleaner look, faster navigation, and a few rough edges sanded down along the way.

Every page now shares one consistent design system, so things like buttons, status tags, and navigation look and behave the same no matter where you are on the site. We also fixed a handful of small bugs: a broken icon on the Helpline page, a couple of typos on the Management page, and some inconsistent styling between pages.

It's a behind-the-scenes update, but it sets us up to ship news, games, and updates faster going forward. As always, thoughts and bug reports are welcome on Discord.
