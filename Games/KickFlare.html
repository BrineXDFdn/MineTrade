KickFlare brings classic football action to the 2D field with simple controls and fast-paced gameplay. This PC-exclusive single-player game currently features the special "KickFlare" mode, designed to test your skill and strategy against AI opponents. Even in the Alpha version, the game delivers a smooth and exciting football experience.
